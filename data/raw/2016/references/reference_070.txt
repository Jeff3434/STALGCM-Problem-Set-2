Binay's running mate 'anyone but Trillanes'
By Patricia Lourdes Viray
(philstar.com)
|
Updated May 19, 2015 - 4:32pm
Sen. Antonio Trillanes IV has been aggressively pursuing corruption allegations against Vice President Jejomar Binay (pictured).
OVP
MANILA, Philippines - There are a lot of possibilities when it comes Vice President Jejomar Binay's running mate in the forthcoming May 2016 national elections but Makati Rep. Abigail Binay has one request to her father.
"Hindi niya pwede kunin si Trillanes [na] vice president, 'yun lang. Kunin niya na lahat ng tao 'wag lang si Trillanes," Abigail said during a live interview on ANC on Monday.
Sen. Antonio Trillanes IV has been throwing numerous corruption allegations to the Binay family at Senate Blue Ribbon subcommittee hearings in recent months.

Earlier this year, Trillanes challenged the elder Binay to a public debate which the latter accepted and eventually
backed out
from.
Sen. Grace Poe and Sen. Francis Escudero are among the rumored possible running mates for the elder Binay come 2016.

"Why not Grace Poe? That's the most ideal. Binay-Poe is the most ideal," Abigail said.
Following reports that the Liberal Party is pursuing her to run for president, Poe admitted on Monday that she prefers Escudero as her running mate.
The popular senator has not yet decided on her plans for the 2016 polls despite her previous meeting with President Benigno Aquino III regarding the elections.
