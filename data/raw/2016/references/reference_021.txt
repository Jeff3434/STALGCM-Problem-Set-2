Trillanes inspects another alleged ‘overpriced’ Binay building–Makati Science High School
By:
Nestor Corrales
-  Reporter                           
                         /
@NCorralesINQ
INQUIRER.net
/ 10:49 AM November 15, 2014
Share:
Photo by RYAN LEAGOGO/INQUIRER.net
MANILA, Philippines — The senate blue ribbon subcommittee on Saturday conducted an ocular inspection of the Makati Science High School allegedly overpriced and built during the stint of Vice President Jejomar Binay as city mayor.
Senators Antonio Trillanes IV and Aquilino Pimentel III led the inspection of the 10-story high school building  after lawyer Renato Bondal revealed in a Senate hearing that it was overpriced by P800 million or 300 percent.

Trillanes led the ocular inspection of the allegedly overpriced Makati City Parking Building and the  “Hacienda Binay” in Rosario, Batangas, which Binay allegedly acquired through dummy corporations.
