LOOK: Comelec releases certified list of presidential, VP bets
By:
Yuji Vincent Gonzales
-  Reporter                           
                         /
@YGonzalesINQ
INQUIRER.net
/ 01:50 PM February 05, 2016
Share:
PHOTO FROM COMMISSION ON ELECTIONS
The Commission on Elections (Comelec) on Friday released the certified list of names of presidential and vice presidential candidates for the upcoming national polls that will appear on the ballot.
It will be a six-cornered fight for the two highest political posts in the country.
Article continues after this advertisement
Six candidates are vying for the presidency, namely opposition leader Vice President Jejomar Binay, Sen. Miriam Defensor-Santiago, Davao City Mayor Rodrigo Duterte of Partido Demokratiko Pilipino-Lakas ng Bayan, Sen. Grace Poe (independent), administration bet Manuel “Mar” Roxas II (Liberal Party), and OFW Family Club party-list Rep. Roy Señeres (Partido ng Manggagawa at Magsasaka).

Six names also appeared on the certified list for vice presidential candidates, namely Sen. Alan Peter Cayetano (independent), Sen. Francis “Chiz” Escudero (independent), Sen. Gringo Honasan (UNA), Sen. Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos Jr. (independent), Camarines Sur Rep. Leni Robredo (LP), and Sen. Antonio Trillanes IV (independent).
The poll body previously said it will start the printing of ballots to be used in the May elections on Feb. 8.
